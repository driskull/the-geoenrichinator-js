Help!

  1. Create a webmap containing a feature layer with at least one polygon to enrich. (Include the full URL to the layer ID)
  2. Enter the address of your portal and webmap ID, and hit "Go!"
  3. Select the impact layer, optionally enter your desired geoenrichment variables, and select "Feed Me!"
  4. Once completed, select "Open in new webmap", disable the popup, modify the renderer, and share it publicly.
  5. Add the webmap ID to the URL of the LIM. (tmappsevents.esri.com/website/LIM?webmap=<webmap ID>)
